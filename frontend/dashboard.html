About CareBot

CareBot is an AI-based health recommendation system designed to provide guidance based on basic symptoms entered by users. It analyzes common health indicators and suggests preventive steps and possible care measures.

Disclaimer: CareBot is not a licensed medical professional and does not replace consultation with a qualified doctor. For emergency conditions, consult a healthcare provider immediately.
